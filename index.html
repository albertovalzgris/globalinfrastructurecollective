About

The Global Infrastructure Research Collective is an international academic network of geographers and urban scholars focusing on global infrastructure initiatives and their urban dimensions. Our research integrates the study of infrastructure with urban development, addressing the complexities of contemporary urbanization. We are dedicated to advancing knowledge, fostering critical thinking, and promoting transformative urban practice for a different world. Our collective is committed to providing a platform for interdisciplinary research and collaboration, shaping the international debate around global infrastructure.
